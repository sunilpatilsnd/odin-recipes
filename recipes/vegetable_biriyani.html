Vegetable Biryani

Description

Fragrant basmati rice cooked with mixed vegetables and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, beans)
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Cashews and raisins for garnish
  • Fresh mint and coriander for flavor

Steps

  1. Saut√© onions until golden, add ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add tomatoes, vegetables, and spices. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Mix in yogurt and rice. Add water and cook until rice is done.
  4. Garnish with mint, coriander, cashews, and raisins. Serve hot.
