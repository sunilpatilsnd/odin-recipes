Chicken Curry

Description

A classic Indian dish with tender chicken pieces in a flavorful curry sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Saut√© onions until golden, add ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add pureed tomatoes, cook until oil separates.
  3. Add chicken, yogurt, and spices. Cook until chicken is tender.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander. Serve hot with rice or naan.
